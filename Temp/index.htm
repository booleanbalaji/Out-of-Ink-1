About

Out of Ink

All the wizards of words and witches of drama, lend me your senses for this is an opportunity to create magic which you might not wanna miss; not with wands, but with pens. A chance to showcase how well you can play with the human psyche to create a web of gripping storyline with intriguing characters or a field of fun and colourful comic strip or a drama so intense, it ceases the heart-beat of the reader. Let’s cut the chase and get to the point. I present to you-“Out of INK”.

A first of its kind Literary Hackfest which provides you the platform to showcase your abilities to express in different forms. With English creating its prominence in India and with more literary enthusiasts coming in the fray, we decided to create a forum to showcase the same interests through a competition. This Literary hackfest aims at surfacing those talents among youth and elders alike and allowing them to stand together to compete for becoming the first Literary Genius of India. We never believed in age as a measure of talent and thus however old you might be or however young, let go of the Shakespeare inside.

This Hack fest is a celebration of Literature not only through classical forms of Novels and poetry but also through Comics and Drama. We aim to achieve this by providing you 24hours of time limit to finish your piece on the spot under any of the previously mentioned genres. We believe Literature is in the mere expression of thoughts and imagination, limitless of the form it is realized in. And with this belief, the competition will be starred with guest lectures, with special invitations to prominent personalities of the worlds of literature, big publishing houses along with a cultural evening and much, much more.

So bringing to you- “Out Of Ink” for the very first time in VIT University as a Literary hack fest presented to you by CSED and The Hindu Education club in collaboration. Get your ink bottles ready along with your Quills, for the time is now to let them ideas flow without restrictions and create a masterpiece for people to awe upon.
